About ZT Controls

ZT Controls Inc., founded by Zheming Tang, P.Eng., provides specialized technical consulting services for gas turbine control systems, GE Mark VI/VIe platforms, power plant operations, and outage support. With more than 30 years of engineering and field experience, ZT Controls delivers reliable, expert-level support to utilities and industrial clients across North America.
