About ZT Controls

ZT Controls Inc., founded by Zheming Tang, P.Eng., provides expert support for gas turbine control systems, power plant automation, and operational reliability. Based in Alberta and serving clients across North America, ZT Controls specializes in GE Mark V, Mark VI, and Mark VIe platforms as well as Ex2000/2100/2100e excitation systems.

Our focus is delivering practical, field‑proven engineering solutions that improve equipment performance, plant safety, and system reliability.

Mission Statement: To provide reliable, expert gas turbine and power plant control support that enhances performance and ensures safe, dependable operation.
